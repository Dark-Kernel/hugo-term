Combine audio & video of different length

2024-06-24Sumit Patel
#ffmpeg 
  1. Long audio & shortvideo:
ffmpeg -i Downloads/pooja_updated.mp4 -i aum.opus -map 0:v:0 -map 1:a:0 -c:v copy -shortest -y output3.mp4
